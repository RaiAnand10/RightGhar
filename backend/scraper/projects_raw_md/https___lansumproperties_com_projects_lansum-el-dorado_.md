#### Closer To The Financial District

Living closer to the Financial District(14 mins away) means getting access to a plethora of services. It means you will live closer to the IT/ITES capital of Hyderabad

![](https://lansumproperties.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Artboard-9-scaled.jpg.webp)

#### Luxury Amenities that redefine quality living

With El Dorado, you are getting access to the exclusive 6 lavish floors of a luxurious clubhouse. You get a 2 basement floor dedicated to badminton and squash courts.

![](https://lansumproperties.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Artboard-3-15-scaled.jpg.webp)

El Dorado is thoughtfully designed, keeping the environment in mind. Inspired by biophilic design, experience a flow of greenery in every corner of the community.

![](https://lansumproperties.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Artboard-4-15-scaled.jpg)
