### What is Rajapushpa Imperia and where is it located? ![](https://www.rajapushpa.in/projects/residential/imperia-assets/images/amenities-icons/arrow-up-light.png)

Located in the bustling locales of Tellapur, near Financial District, Rajapushpa IMPERIA is a premium lifestyle residential project. It is a beautifully planned gated community with vastu-compliant 2 BHK Apartments in Tellapur & 3 BHK Apartments in Tellapur. Spacious, breezy and designed to perfection - these apartments are meticulously engineered. Rajapushpa Imperia is a home for everyone where you can Experience an Elevated Lifestyle. LIVE LARGE every day, every moment.

### What is the total land area of the project? ![](https://www.rajapushpa.in/projects/residential/imperia-assets/images/amenities-icons/arrow-up-light.png)

Spread over an expansive 24 acres of land, this project comprises spacious and well-laid apartments within 8 magnificent towers. These high-rise residential towers go up to 40 floors and house lavish 2 & 3 BHK apartments with airy sundeck balconies. The project has more than 82% open space dedicated to extensive landscaping and various sports facilities.

### What are the different types and sizes of apartments? ![](https://www.rajapushpa.in/projects/residential/imperia-assets/images/amenities-icons/arrow-up-light.png)

Rajapushpa Imperia offers a total of 3008 high rise apartments with 10 apartment units on each floor. These apartments are spacious and well-designed in 2 BHK & 3 BHK dimensions.
