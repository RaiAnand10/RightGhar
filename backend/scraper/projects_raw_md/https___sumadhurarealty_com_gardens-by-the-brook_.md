## Overview

Find your ideal investment solution with Gardens by the Brook by Sumadhura – **luxury apartments in Hyderabad** highly positioned only 10 minutes from the airport and 20 minutes from the Financial District.

Embrace nature within this uncommon low-density complex consisting of 2 acres of vast open areas and green spaces, redefining high-rise residential living in South Hyderabad. Our well-planned **2 & 3 BHK luxury apartments** ensure greater privacy yet provide a unified experience with nature around you.

Gardens by the Brook breaks the mold of ordinary living areas to provide an oasis wherein luxury and peace coexist in perfect harmony. Available for occupancy starting September 2025, our upscale gated community invites residents to witness a singular, premium living experience.
