## FAQs

### What is Aparna Deccan Town?

Aparna Deccan Town is the ‘Township of Tomorrow’ - An expansive township project comprising of 4 high-rise apartment gated communities and a gated enclave of 99 premium bungalows.

### Where is Aparna Deccan Town located?

It is situated in the Gopanpally-Gachibowli area of Hyderabad, offering excellent connectivity to major IT hubs and commercial centers.

### Which projects are part of Aparna Deccan Town?

Aparna Deccan Town currently features Aparna Sunstone and Aparna Amber Bungalows project/s, with more exciting developments coming soon.
