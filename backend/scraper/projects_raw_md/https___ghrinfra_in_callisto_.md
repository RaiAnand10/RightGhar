### SMART FEATURES

##### Beyond Ordinary Living

GHR Callisto is an 8.3-acre gated community comprising stunning 2, 2.5, 3 & 4 BHK apartments. At GHR Infra, we are committed to our philosophy of ‘smart life in nature’ and have created a community that provides a sophisticated and soulful living experience to all our residents.

##### Sustainable Living

GHR Callisto is an IGBC-certified community providing a perfect mix of style and sustainability. Through measures like organic waste composting, and rainwater harvesting among others lead a life of luxury and serenity in an environmentally friendly way.
