### VASAVI ANANDA NILAYAM

### Project Overview

Expediency like never before! Ananda Nilayam created a new era in the evolution of the gated communities in India. With a skyline directly connecting to the Vasavi L.B. Nagar metro station, experience the comfort & convenience at your doorstep. Set amidst lush greenery & picturesque landscapes, this idyllic residential haven offers tranquility. All-feature-packed 2, 3 & 4 BHK apartments & sky villas are equipped with amenities that are a testament to its commitment to providing a lifestyle of convenience & indulgence. Revel in the splendor of a shimmering swimming pool, unwind in the serene gardens, or partake in recreational activities at the well-equipped clubhouse. Ananda Nilayam stands as an epitome of gated community villas of Hyderabad
