## SPECIFICATIONS

Ceiling finishes

**a) Drawing, Dining, Living, Bedrooms, kitchen and Balcony:** Smoothly finished with putty and acrylic and emulsion paint

**b) Bathrooms:** Grid ceiling to cover all service lines

Wall Finishing

**a) Drawing, Dining, Living, Bedrooms, kitchen and Balcony:** Smoothly finished with putty and acrylic and emulsion paint.

**b) Bathrooms:** Reputed make of GVT/Ceramic tiles cladding up to lintel height.

**c) External Finishing:** Texture Finish & two coats of exterior emulsion paint of reputed brands with architectural features

Flooring

**a) Drawing, Dining, Living, Bedrooms, Kitchen:** 800 X 800 mm size double charged vitrified tiles of reputed brand with spacer joint.

**b)Balcony/Bathrooms/Utility:** Anti-skid vitrified/ceramic tiles

**c) Corridors:** Vitrified tiles with spacer joint

**d) Staircase:** Natural Stone/Granite

Windows/Grills

All Windows are of aluminium alloy/UPVC glazed sliding/openable shutters with EPDM gaskets, necessary hardware with M.S Grill and provision for mosquito mesh shutter

Waterproofing

Waterproofing shall be provided for all bathrooms, balconies, utility area and roof terrace

Fire Safety

Fire alarm, automatic sprinklers and wet risers as per Fire Authority Regulations

LPG/PNG & WTP & STP

Supply of LPG/PNG with piped gas system

**Lifts:** V3F drive high speed lifts of reputed make. Lift lobby cladding with vitrified tiles/granite.

- Water treatment plant for bore-well water and water meter for each unit
- A Sewage treatment plant of adequate capacity as per norms inside the project
- Treated sewage water for the landscape and flushing purpose.

Super Structure & Walls

RCC shear wall-framed structure, resistant to wind and earthquake (Zone – 2)

**External Walls :** Reinforced shear wall

**Internal Walls :** Reinforced shear wall

Doors

**a) Main Door:** Hard wood frame, finished with melamine spray polish. Teak finished flush shutters with reputed hardware

**b) Internal Doors:** Hard wood frame or factory made wooden frame with both side laminated flush shutter with reputed hardware

**c) Bathrooms/Utility Doors:** Granite frame with both side laminated flush shutter with reputed hardware.

**d) Balconies:** Aluminum/UPVC glazed french sliding doors with mosquito mesh provision

All Bathrooms

a) Vanity type wash basin with single lever basin mixer

b) EWC with flush tank of the reputed brand

c) Single lever bath and shower mixer

d) Provision for geysers in all bathrooms

e) All faucets are chrome plated of reputed brands

Kitchen

a) Granite platform with single bowl stainless steel sink

b) Provision for water purifier

Electrical

a) Concealed copper wiring of reputed make

b) Power outlet for air conditioners in all bedrooms and living

c) Power outlet for geysers in all bathrooms and utility.

d) Power outlet for chimney, hob, refrigerator, microwave oven, mixer/grinder in kitchen

e) Washing machine and dishwasher point in utility area

f) Three phase supply for each unit and individual prepaid meters

g) Miniature circuit breaker(MCB) for each distribution board of reputed make.

h) Modular switches of reputed make

TV/Telephone

a) Telephone point in drawing and master bedroom

b) Intercom facility to all units connecting security

c) Provision for cable TV connection in drawing, living and all bedrooms

d) One internet connection provision in drawing, living and all bedrooms

Security

- Sophisticated round the security/surveillance system
- Surveillance cameras at the main security and at the entrance of the block & lift cabin

Power Backup

100% DG backup with acoustic enclosure and AMF

Billing System

Automated prepaid billing system for water, power, gas & maintenance.
