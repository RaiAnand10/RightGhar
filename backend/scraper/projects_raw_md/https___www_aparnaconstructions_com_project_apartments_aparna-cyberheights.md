## FAQs

### What is Aparna CyberHeights?

Aparna CyberHeights is a premium residential project offering 2, 2.5, and 3 BHK gated community apartments in Osman Nagar, Hyderabad. Set amidst serene surroundings, it is part of the expansive Aparna Cyber4 Hub, which also includes Aparna CyberOn, Aparna CyberShine, and Aparna CyberStar.

### Where is Aparna CyberHeights located?

Aparna CyberHeights, part of the Aparna Cyber4 Hub is located in Osman Nagar, in the heart of Hyderabad. These projects in Osman Nagar offer a variety of sophisticated, remarkable amenities that are curated to enhance the lifestyle.

### What are the locational advantages of Aparna CyberHeights?

With the sprawling IT culture, splendid infrastructure, and strategic placement, these Gated Communities in Osman Nagar are all set to offer a lifestyle that is futuristic.
