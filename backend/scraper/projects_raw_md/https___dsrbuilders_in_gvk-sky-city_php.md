### ABOUT PROJECT

GVK Skycity is bundled in a neighbourhood that brings the best
of Hyderabad to your doorstep. Overlooking the Hussain Sagar lake and next to Raj Bhavan,
Skycity is 5 to 10 mins away from an assortment of shopping & entertainment malls, boutique
stores, 5-star hotels and prominent parks to browse at your whim.Live stylishly and luxuriously.
Experience the very pinnacle of comfort.
Right in the heart of town.
Words can do little justice to the experience.
