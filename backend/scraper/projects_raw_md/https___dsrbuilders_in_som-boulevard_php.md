### ABOUT PROJECT

Presenting the finest villas in the lap of mother nature, Som
Boulevard is spread across 40 acres of land comprising 270 aesthetically designed villas.
Som Boulevard allows you to unwind in the lap of mother nature. Its design and innovative
construction are built for an attractive living experience.
