## About Prestige Clairemont

Prestige Clairemont is located in Neopolis, a newly established, futuristic, 500+ acre layout in Kokapet, which extends from the financial district.

Neopolis has been planned by the Telangana Government to set a benchmark for the whole country for advanced infrastructural planning and smart living. This is where future Hyderabad is taking shape.

With no zonal and height restrictions, it is a self-contained neighbourhood with a superb mix of commercial and premium residential developments, intelligently situated to be accessible without coming in each other’s way.
