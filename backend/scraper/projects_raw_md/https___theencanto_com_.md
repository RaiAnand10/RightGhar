### Location Advantage

Located in Puppalaguda, Financial District, ENCANTO offers seamless access via ORR and link road to top IT hubs, schools, hospitals, and lifestyle spots in Hyderabad.

![](https://theencanto.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Unit-Layout.png)

### Optimised & Thoughtful Unit Layout

Spanning 4,055–5,045 sq. ft., each home at ENCANTO maximizes space with smart layouts that merge elegance and utility, offering comfort and luxury in every square foot.

![](https://theencanto.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Architectural-Elegance.png)

### Architectural Elegance

ENCANTO’s bold facade with premium finishes reflects refined living. Sharp lines and modern design make it a standout icon in West Hyderabad’s skyline.

![](https://theencanto.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Flowing-landscape.png)

### Flowing landscape & Amenities

A five-layered landscape with terrace decks, wraparound balconies, and lush sit-outs blends indoors with outdoors, offering serene views and vibrant amenities at every level.
