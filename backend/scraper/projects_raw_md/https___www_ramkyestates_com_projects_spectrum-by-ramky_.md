### PROJECT OVERVIEW

An exclusive gated community of 151 luxurious 4 BHK triplex villas spread across 13.38 acres in Korremula, East Hyderabad. Thoughtfully crafted to harmonize elegance, comfort, and sustainability, The [Spectrum](https://spectrumbyramky.com/) offers villa plots ranging from 181 to 467 sq. yds., with super built-up areas between 2,472 and 2,913 sq.ft.

Each villa features 10-ft ceiling heights, dedicated car parking, EV charging point, solar power provision, and 1 kW backup, ensuring a smart, eco-friendly lifestyle.

Residents enjoy world-class recreation through a 16,830 sft clubhouse equipped with a swimming pool, banquet hall, gym, yoga room, indoor badminton court, gaming zones, guest rooms, and business facilities. Outdoor amenities include tennis, pickleball, volleyball, and basketball courts, mini golf, 14 themed parks, yoga lawns, party lawns, a pet park, and children’s play zones.

Ideally located just 5 minutes from the ORR, near Infosys SEZ and Raheja Mindspace, The [Spectrum](https://spectrumbyramky.com/) offers unmatched connectivity to leading schools, universities, and lifestyle destinations — making it one of East Hyderabad’s most desirable villa communities.
