# Rajapushpa Serene Dale

## Overview
Rajapushpa Serene Dale is a premium villa community located in Tellapur, Hyderabad, spread across a sprawling 28.67 acres. Offering 254 meticulously designed 4 & 5 BHK villas, this gated community blends urban living with lush natural surroundings. With a grand 32,000 sq. ft. clubhouse and 48% open space, Serene Dale redefines luxury living.
