# GHR Callisto

## Overview
GHR Callisto by GHR Infra is a premium gated community located in Kollur, Hyderabad. Spanning across 8.3 acres, this IGBC Pre-Certified Gold Rated project offers a blend of smart and sustainable living with 2, 2.5, 3, and 4 BHK apartments. Designed to provide a sophisticated lifestyle, GHR Callisto integrates modern technology with eco-friendly practices.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Kollur, Hyderabad, offering excellent connectivity to key areas.
- **Sustainable Living**: IGBC Pre-Certified Gold Rated with features like rainwater harvesting, organic waste composting, and rooftop solar panels.
- **Smart Home Features**: Includes lighting and fan automation, remote access, and water-efficient fixtures.
- **Spacious Clubhouse**: A 50,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art amenities.
- **Variety of Configurations**: Offers 2, 2.5, 3, and 4 BHK apartments ranging from 1195 to 3585 Sq. Ft.
