# Rajapushpa Aurelia

## Overview
Rajapushpa Aurelia is a luxurious residential project located in the serene locales of Tellapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 12.5 acres, this gated community offers meticulously designed 3 & 4 BHK apartments that blend space, luxury, and modern living. With 7 high-rise towers featuring 56 floors, the project is a true symphony of elegance and comfort.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Tellapur, near the Financial District.
- Spacious 3 & 4 BHK corner apartments with sizes ranging from 3015 to 4400 sq. ft.
- High-rise towers with 56 floors offering breathtaking views.
- Grand clubhouse spanning 76,500 sq. ft. with state-of-the-art amenities.
- Vastu-compliant design and ample open spaces.
- Proximity to schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and IT hubs.

## Location Highlights
- **Connectivity**: Located in Tellapur, with easy access to the Financial District (20 mins), Outer Ring Road (15 mins), and Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (40 mins).
- **Nearby Schools**: Glendale International School (10 mins), Manthan International School (15 mins), Oakridge International School.
- **Healthcare**: Citizens Hospital (20 mins), Continental Hospital (25 mins).
- **Shopping & Entertainment**: AMB Mall (30 mins), Inorbit Mall, Sarath City Capital Mall.
- **IT Hubs**: Microsoft, Wipro, Amazon Development Centre, and Google within 20-30 mins drive.

Rajapushpa Aurelia offers a perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and connectivity, making it an ideal choice for modern living.
