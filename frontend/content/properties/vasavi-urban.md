# Vasavi Urban

## Overview

Vasavi Urban is a premium residential project offering meticulously designed 2 BHK apartments in the serene locality of Bachupally, Hyderabad. Spread across 17.86 acres, this gated community combines modern aesthetics with lush green spaces, ensuring a perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and tranquility. With a focus on safety and convenience, Vasavi Urban is an ideal choice for young professionals and families seeking a contemporary lifestyle.

## Standout Features

- Located in the fast-developing area of Bachupally, Hyderabad.
- A sprawling 17.86-acre gated community with 70% open space.
- 12 towers with 23 floors each, housing a total of 3714 units.
- Massive 1,00,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art facilities.
- RERA-approved project (RERA No.: P02200002275).
