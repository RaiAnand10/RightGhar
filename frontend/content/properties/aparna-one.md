# Aparna One

## Overview
Aparna One is an ultra-luxurious gated community located in Shaikpet, Hyderabad. Offering 3 and 4 BHK apartments, this project is designed for those who seek an elite lifestyle with world-class amenities, expansive living spaces, and a prime location near Hyderabad's most upscale neighborhoods.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Shaikpet, close to Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, HITEC City, and Gachibowli.
- **Exclusive Living**: A gated community designed for the elite, offering a royal lifestyle.
- **Spacious Apartments**: Unit sizes ranging from 2876 to 5216 sq. ft.
- **World-Class Amenities**: Over 40 premium amenities, including a state-of-the-art clubhouse.
- **Sustainability**: IGBC Platinum Rated Green Building.
- **Private Spaces**: Apartments with private lobbies, home theatres, maid rooms, and private garden seating for 4BHK units.
- **Ample Parking**: 2-4 car parks per apartment.
- **Breathtaking Views**: Overlooks Golconda Fort, Hyderabad Golf Club, and Qutb Shahi Tombs.
