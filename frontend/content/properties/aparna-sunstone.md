# Aparna Sunstone

## Overview
Aparna Sunstone is a premium 3 BHK high-rise apartment project located in Gopanpally, Hyderabad, within the Aparna Deccan Town township. Spread across 24.9 acres, this luxurious gated community offers 3213 units designed to provide an elevated lifestyle with world-class amenities and vibrant community spaces.

## Standout Features
- Located in the thriving growth corridor of Gopanpally-Gachibowli, Hyderabad.
- Part of Aparna Deccan Town, a futuristic township.
- 70,000+ Sq. Ft. clubhouse with 60+ premium lifestyle amenities.
- 39,000+ Sq. Ft. dedicated sports and commercial block.
- 3.2 acres of landscaped open space with pedestrian-friendly pathways.
- Vibrant common zones fostering community connections.
- Dedicated 100 ft. access roads for seamless connectivity.
- High-rise towers with G+44 floors and 8 high-speed elevators.
