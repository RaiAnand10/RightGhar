# Aparna Greenscapes

## Overview
Aparna Greenscapes is a premium gated community offering luxurious 3, 4, and 5 BHK apartments in Kompally, Hyderabad. Spread across 13 acres, this project combines nature-inspired living with modern conveniences, ensuring a serene yet connected lifestyle. With 7 towers featuring G+28 floors, Aparna Greenscapes redefines upscale living.

## Standout Features
- Excellent connectivity to Central and North Hyderabad.
- Quick access to ORR Exit 6 and NH-44.
- Located on Kompally Main Road, minutes away from Suchitra Junction.
- Overlooks the picturesque Fox Sagar Lake.
- Low-density living with just 50 units per acre.
- Expansive apartments with an extra maid room.
- Features a VR gaming room and state-of-the-art clubhouse.
- Abundant landscaped areas and open spaces.
