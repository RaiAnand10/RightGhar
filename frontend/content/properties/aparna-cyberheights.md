# Aparna CyberHeights

## Overview
Aparna CyberHeights is a premium gated community project located in Osman Nagar, Hyderabad. Spread across 6.68 acres, this development offers 2, 2.5, and 3 BHK apartments designed for modern living. With 5 towers, 22 floors, and a total of 708 units, the project is an ideal choice for tech professionals and families seeking comfort, convenience, and connectivity.

## Standout Features
- **State-of-the-Art Clubhouse**: A sprawling 37,593 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with 20+ premium amenities.
- **Prime Location**: Situated near Gachibowli, Kollur, HITEC City, and the Financial District.
- **Excellent Connectivity**: 100 Ft. roads leading to Gopanpally Junction and ORR Service Road.
- **Future IT Hub**: Opposite TSIIC land slated for future IT development.
- **Green Living**: Surrounded by greenery and away from city noise.
- **Optimal Design**: Apartments with ample natural light, ventilation, and premium fittings.
