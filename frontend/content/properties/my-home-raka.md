# My Home Raka

## Overview
My Home Raka is a premium residential project located in Madeenaguda, Hyderabad. Spread across 13.53 acres, this standalone G+34 tower offers 300 thoughtfully designed 3 BHK homes ranging from 1969 to 2806 sq. ft. With a focus on luxury and comfort, the project promises a refined lifestyle complemented by modern amenities and lush green surroundings.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Madeenaguda, with seamless connectivity to Gachibowli and HITEC City.
- **Exclusive Design**: A single G+34 tower offering privacy and exclusivity.
- **Spacious Units**: 3 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 1969 to 2806 sq. ft.
- **Open Space**: 81.6% open space for a serene and unpolluted living environment.
- **Premium Lifestyle**: Designed for families to create cherished memories with luxurious amenities.
