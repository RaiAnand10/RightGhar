# Rajapushpa Imperia

## Overview
Rajapushpa Imperia is a premium gated community offering 2 & 3 BHK lifestyle apartments located in the rapidly growing neighborhood of Tellapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 24 acres, this project features 8 towers with 40 floors, over 82% open spaces, and a grand 1.2 lakh sq. ft. clubhouse. Designed to provide a luxurious and serene living experience, it is set to redefine fine living in the Financial District.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Tellapur, Hyderabad, near the Financial District.
- Spacious 2 & 3 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 1350 to 2880 sq. ft.
- 8 high-rise towers with 40 floors each.
- Over 82% open space for a greener and healthier lifestyle.
- Grand clubhouse spanning 1.2 lakh sq. ft. with premium amenities.
- Electric car charging provisions and centralized piped LPG.
- Sundeck apartments offering stunning views of the urban landscape.
- Starting price of ₹1.10 Cr onwards.
