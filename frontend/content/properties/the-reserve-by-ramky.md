# The Reserve by Ramky

## Overview
The Reserve by Ramky is a premium villa community spread across 19.2 acres in Harshaguda, South Hyderabad. Designed to offer a harmonious blend of modern luxury and serene countryside living, the project is strategically located near Rajiv Gandhi International Airport and upcoming infrastructure developments. The villas are thoughtfully designed with Vaastu compliance, private gardens, and spacious layouts ranging from 5,500 to 8,000 sq. ft., making it an ideal choice for luxury living.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Harshaguda, South Hyderabad, with proximity to Rajiv Gandhi International Airport and major infrastructure projects.
- **Exclusive Community**: Limited to just 99 luxury villas, ensuring privacy and exclusivity.
- **Themed Parks**: Features 10 themed pocket parks connected by lush green corridors and a central loop road for safety and privacy.
- **Luxury Villas**: 4 & 5 BHK villas with contemporary architecture, premium finishes, private gardens, and double-height dining areas.
- **World-Class Amenities**: Two clubhouses spanning 50,000 sq. ft. with pools, sports courts, wellness spaces, and social hubs.
