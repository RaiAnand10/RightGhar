# Prestige Clairemont

## Overview
Prestige Clairemont is a premium residential project located in the futuristic Neopolis layout of Kokapet, Hyderabad. Designed to redefine urban living, this project offers luxurious 3 & 4 BHK apartments with modern amenities and excellent connectivity to the financial district.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located in Neopolis, Kokapet, a well-planned 500+ acre layout by the Telangana Government.
- No zonal or height restrictions, ensuring a mix of commercial and premium residential developments.
- Close proximity to Hyderabad's financial district and major IT hubs.
- Spacious apartments with sizes ranging from 1989 to 4060 Sq. Ft.
