# My Home Grava

## Overview
My Home Grava is a luxurious residential project located in the prestigious Neopolis, Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spread across 17.52 acres with 80% open space, the project features seven magnificent towers, including one towering at Ground + 54 floors, redefining the skyline. Designed for affluent families, these 4 BHK residences offer expansive living spaces, private home theatres, cascading balconies, and panoramic views.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Neopolis, Kokapet, Hyderabad.
- Seven towers with Ground + 54 floors and Ground + 49 floors.
- Spacious 4 BHK residences ranging from 4365 to 8640 sq. ft.
- 1,20,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with world-class amenities.
- 80% open space for a serene and expansive living experience.
- TS RERA registered (P02400007342).
