# Rajapushpa Pristinia

## Overview
Rajapushpa Pristinia is an ongoing premium residential project in Kokapet, Hyderabad, offering thoughtfully designed 3 & 4 BHK apartments. Spread across 12.1 acres, the project comprises six towers with a total of 1782 units, featuring modern architecture and luxurious amenities. With its strategic location near Neopolis SEZ and excellent connectivity to key areas like Gachibowli and Financial District, this project promises a perfect blend of convenience and luxury.

## Standout Features
- Located in Kokapet, Hyderabad, near Neopolis SEZ.
- 6 towers with 42 and 38 floors, housing 1782 apartments.
- Spacious units ranging from 2040 to 4595 sq. ft.
- 60,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with world-class amenities.
- 80% open spaces with lush green landscaping.
- Proximity to Financial District, Gachibowli, and Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.
- Hyderabad's first stilt-level floor with amenities.

## Amenities
### Clubhouse
