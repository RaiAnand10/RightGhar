# Rajapushpa Casa Luxura

## Overview
Rajapushpa Casa Luxura is the crown jewel of Neopolis, Hyderabad, offering ultra-luxury living in the city's most sought-after urban hub. Spread across 7.7 acres with 72% open space, this project features five iconic towers soaring 50 floors high, housing expansive 4 BHK residences with premium finishes, elevated ceiling heights, and breathtaking views. Designed for the discerning homeowner, Casa Luxura redefines high-rise living with its world-class amenities and strategic location near the Financial District, IT hubs, and Outer Ring Road (ORR).

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Neopolis, Hyderabad's future-ready urban hub, with seamless connectivity to the Financial District, IT hubs, and ORR.
- **Architectural Excellence**: Five iconic towers with 50 floors, offering a stunning skyline view.
- **Spacious Residences**: Ultra-luxury 4 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 5735 to 7790 Sq. Ft.
- **Expansive Open Spaces**: 72% open space for a serene and tranquil living experience.
- **Luxury Clubhouse**: A 60,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with premium lifestyle amenities.
- **Sky Deck Amenities**: 35,000 Sq. Ft. of sky-level experiences, offering panoramic views and exclusive indulgences.
