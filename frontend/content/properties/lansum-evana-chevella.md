# Lansum Evana Chevella

## Overview
Lansum Evana Chevella is a thoughtfully designed residential project nestled in the serene surroundings of Chevella, Hyderabad. Spread across 34 acres, this premium development features 251 meticulously crafted 3 BHK villas with private basements, offering a harmonious blend of nature, luxury, and convenience.

## Standout Features
- **Location**: Situated in Chevella, a growing residential hub in West Hyderabad, with excellent connectivity to key areas like Financial District, Gachibowli, and RGIA.
- **Lifestyle**: 34-acre vehicle-free podium with basement parking, ensuring safety and tranquility for residents.
- **Design**: Villas with seamless indoor-outdoor connections, mindful layouts, and natural textures for a soothing living experience.
- **Community Spaces**: Decentralized spaces including meditation gardens, hammock zones, bamboo groves, and edible gardens.
- **Nature Integration**: 5 landscaped zones with 4 km of paved walkways, offering diverse natural experiences.

## Amenities
