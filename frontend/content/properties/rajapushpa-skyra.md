# Rajapushpa Skyra

## Overview
Rajapushpa Skyra is an ultra-luxury residential project located in Neopolis, Hyderabad. Spread across 6.5 acres with 80% open spaces, this premium development offers 3 and 4 BHK residences designed to elevate your lifestyle. With 56 floors across 3 iconic towers, the project redefines luxury living with spacious apartments, state-of-the-art amenities, and breathtaking views of the city skyline.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Neopolis, a rapidly growing upscale neighborhood in West Hyderabad.
- **Architectural Marvel**: 3 iconic towers with 56 floors of sky-high living.
- **Spacious Residences**: 3 & 4 BHK apartments ranging from 3140 to 5350 Sq. Ft.
- **Luxury Clubhouse**: A massive 1,15,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with lifestyle amenities.
- **Sky Amenities**: Exclusive rooftop features like sky yoga deck, sky cinema, and terrace party lawn.
- **Sustainability**: 80% open spaces and eco-friendly design elements.
