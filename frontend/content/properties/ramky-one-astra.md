# Ramky One Astra

## Overview
Ramky One Astra is a premium residential project located in the prime locality of Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spread across 3.69 acres, this gated community offers 292 luxurious 3 & 4 BHK apartments designed to provide a perfect blend of modern amenities and serene green surroundings. Developed by the renowned Ramky Estates, this project is an ideal choice for those seeking a tranquil yet connected lifestyle.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located on the ORR Service Road in Kokapet, offering excellent connectivity.
- Proximity to the Financial District and Kokapet ORR exits.
- Spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 1720 to 2460 Sq. Ft.
