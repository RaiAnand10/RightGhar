# ENCANTO

## Overview
ENCANTO by Lansum & MK is an ultra-luxury gated community located in the heart of Puppalaguda, Financial District, Hyderabad. Offering 3 & 4 BHK residences, this project is designed for connoisseurs of fine living, blending timeless architecture, refined taste, and unmatched attention to detail.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Puppalaguda, Financial District, Hyderabad.
- Ultra-luxury 3 & 4 BHK residences with sizes ranging from 4055 to 5045 sq. ft.
- Thoughtfully designed homes with elegant layouts and premium finishes.
- Five-layered landscaped design with terrace decks, wraparound balconies, and lush sit-outs.
- Seamless connectivity via ORR and link roads to IT hubs, schools, hospitals, and lifestyle destinations.
