# Ramky One Odyssey

## Overview
Ramky One Odyssey is a premium residential project located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.3 acres, this luxurious development features 783 meticulously designed apartments in configurations of 3, 3.5, and 4.5 BHK. With 36 floors across 3 high-rise towers, the project offers a blend of modern architecture, spacious layouts, and a host of world-class amenities.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located in Kokapet, Hyderabad, with excellent connectivity to major city hubs.
- Spacious apartments designed with Vastu principles and extravagant balconies.
- 50,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse offering a wide range of lifestyle amenities.
- Close proximity to reputed international schools, multi-speciality hospitals, and the Financial District.
- Just 30 minutes' drive from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (RGIA).
