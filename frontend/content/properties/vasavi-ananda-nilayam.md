# Vasavi Ananda Nilayam

## Overview
Vasavi Ananda Nilayam is a landmark residential project located in L.B. Nagar, Hyderabad. This expansive gated community spans 29.37 acres and offers a variety of 2, 3, 4, and 5 BHK apartments along with luxurious sky villas. Strategically located next to the Vasavi L.B. Nagar Metro Station, the project combines convenience, modern amenities, and lush green surroundings to create a tranquil living experience.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in L.B. Nagar, Hyderabad, with direct connectivity to the Vasavi L.B. Nagar Metro Station.
- **Expansive Community**: Spread across 29.37 acres with 72% open space.
- **Luxury Living**: Offers 2, 3, 4, and 5 BHK apartments along with exclusive sky villas.
- **World-Class Clubhouse**: A massive 131,385 sq. ft. clubhouse with premium facilities.
- **Future-Ready**: Possession scheduled for December 2028.
