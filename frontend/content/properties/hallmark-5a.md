# Hallmark 5A

## Overview
Hallmark 5A is a luxurious gated villa community located in Velimela, Hyderabad, offering ultra-modern 3 & 4 BHK triplex villas. Spread across 5.35 acres, this project is designed for new-age living with a focus on privacy, comfort, and world-class amenities. The project also features a 7,200 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art facilities.

## Standout Features
- Located in Velimela, off Kollur, a rapidly developing area in Hyderabad.
- G+2 triplex villas with ultra-modern designs and premium specifications.
- 7,200 sq. ft. world-class clubhouse with modern amenities.
- Proximity to Financial District, ITIR, and major educational institutions.
- Serene and green surroundings for a peaceful lifestyle.
