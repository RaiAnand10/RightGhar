# Vasavi Avasa

## Overview
Vasavi Avasa is a luxurious gated villa community located in the serene surroundings of Kompally, Hyderabad. Spread across 6.27 acres, the project features 59 meticulously designed 4 BHK villas ranging from 4405 to 4647 square feet. The community boasts a 9198 square feet clubhouse equipped with premium amenities, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a blend of comfort, elegance, and convenience.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Kompally, Hyderabad, offering excellent connectivity.
- Exclusive gated community with only 59 villas for enhanced privacy.
- Spacious villas with sizes ranging from 4405 to 4647 Sq. Ft.
- A 9198 Sq. Ft. clubhouse serving as the social hub of the community.
- Possession scheduled for December 2026 with a 6-month grace period.
