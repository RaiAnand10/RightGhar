# IRIS By Raghava

## Overview
IRIS By Raghava is a luxurious residential project located in the prime area of Raidurgam, Hyderabad. Spanning across 7 acres, this project features three iconic skyscrapers with 45 floors each, offering lavish 4 and 6 BHK apartments. Designed with exclusivity and grandeur in mind, IRIS redefines urban living with its world-class amenities and breathtaking views.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Raidurgam, Hyderabad, offering excellent connectivity.
- Three towering skyscrapers with 45 floors each, providing panoramic lake and city views.
- Spacious 4 and 6 BHK apartments with private elevators for ultimate privacy.
- Exclusive Sky Island connecting all three towers, a unique architectural marvel.
- A 52,000 sq. ft. clubhouse featuring banquet halls, mini-theatres, co-working spaces, cafes, and a rooftop pool.
- Designed with Shear Wall Technology for superior construction quality.
