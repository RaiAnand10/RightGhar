# Candeur Lakescape

## Overview
Welcome to **Candeur Lakescape**, a premium residential project located in Kondapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 9.11 acres, this luxurious development offers thoughtfully designed **2, 2.5, 3 & 3.5 BHK apartments** with stunning views of Gopi Cheruvu Lake. With its modern architecture, expansive clubhouse, and proximity to Hyderabad’s IT hubs, Candeur Lakescape redefines urban living.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Located in Kondapur, just 12 minutes from Gachibowli and close to Hyderabad’s IT hubs like Hitec City and Financial District.
- **Spacious Apartments**: Configurations ranging from 1296 to 2415 sq. ft. with 11-feet ceiling heights for a grand living experience.
- **World-Class Clubhouse**: A 1 Lakh sq. ft. clubhouse offering recreation, wellness, and social spaces.
- **Lake Views**: Panoramic views of Gopi Cheruvu Lake, providing a serene and tranquil environment.
- **Modern Gated Community**: 7 towers rising 47 floors, designed with a focus on comfort, luxury, and lifestyle.
