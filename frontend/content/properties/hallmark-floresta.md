# Hallmark Floresta

## Overview
Hallmark Floresta is a luxurious villa project nestled amidst lush greenery in Pati, Hyderabad. Offering a perfect blend of urban living and natural serenity, these G+2 triplex villas are designed to provide a premium lifestyle. Located just 25 minutes from the Financial District, Floresta combines modernity with tranquility for an unparalleled living experience.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located in Pati, near Patancheru and Kollur, with excellent connectivity to the Financial District and IT hubs.
- Ultra-luxury G+2 triplex villas with spacious 3 & 4 BHK configurations.
- Villas range from 2700 to 4600 Sq. Ft., catering to diverse family needs.
- A sprawling 13.46-acre development surrounded by lush green fields.
- 23,000 Sq. Ft. multi-level clubhouse with state-of-the-art amenities.
- Proximity to top schools, hospitals, and recreational centers.
