# The Prestige City Hyderabad

## Overview
The Prestige City Hyderabad is a luxurious residential project located in Rajendra Nagar, Hyderabad. Featuring 4647 apartments spread across 13 towers with 42 floors, this development offers a new scale of living with unparalleled quality and modern amenities.
