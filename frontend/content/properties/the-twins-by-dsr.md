# The Twins by DSR

## Overview
The Twins by DSR is an ultra-luxury residential project located in Puppalaguda, Hyderabad. Offering a unique living experience with 13 ft ceiling heights and expansive 15,999 sq. ft. apartments, this project redefines opulence. Spread across 3 acres, it features two iconic towers with 44 floors each, housing an exclusive community of just 85 units.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Puppalaguda, just 1 km from Gachibowli and a 30-minute drive to Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.
- **Exclusive Living**: Only one apartment per floor, offering unparalleled privacy and luxury.
- **Architectural Marvel**: South India’s first residential project with a unique glass façade.
- **World-Class Clubhouse**: A sprawling 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with premium amenities.
- **Sustainability Features**: Central vacuum and pest control facilities for a modern lifestyle.
