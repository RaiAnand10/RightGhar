# Honer Signatis

## Overview
Honer Signatis is the largest gated community in the heart of Kukatpally, Hyderabad, offering premium 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK apartments. Spread across 27.5 acres, this project blends modern architecture with lush greenery and top-tier amenities, creating a perfect haven for families seeking comfort and convenience.

## Standout Features
- Prime location at Kukatpally, HITEC City, opposite IDL Lake.
- Spacious apartments ranging from 1695 to 3815 Sq. Ft.
- 18 grand towers with G+25 floors.
- Twin clubhouses spanning 65,000 Sq. Ft. each.
- EV charging stations and temperature-controlled swimming pools.
- RERA-approved project with registration number P02200005923.

## Amenities
### Clubhouse
- Exclusive gyms for men and women.
- Banquet halls and party areas.
- Infinity pool, kids pool, and Jacuzzi.
- Spa and aerobic rooms.
- Art & craft gallery and preview theatre.

### Outdoor
- Tennis courts, badminton courts, squash courts, and basketball courts.

### Convenience
- Conference rooms and guest suites.
- Creche and business lounge.
- Coffee shop and reading lounge.
- Terrace party area and hobby rooms.

## Location Highlights
- Located at IDL Access Rd, Kukatpally, Hyderabad.
- Proximity to major IT hubs like Cyber Towers (6 min, 2.3 km) and Raheja Mindspace (14 min, 4.2 km).
- Nearby malls include Inorbit Mall (18 min, 7.9 km) and Sarath City Capital Mall (19 min, 8.3 km).
- Close to healthcare facilities like Yashoda Hospitals (4 min, 1.3 km) and KIMS Cuddles (4 min, 1.4 km).
