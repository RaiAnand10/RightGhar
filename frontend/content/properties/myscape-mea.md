# Myscape Mea

## Overview
Myscape Mea is an exclusive boutique residential project designed for the discerning few. With only 45 apartments, including 38 spacious units up to 4700 sq. ft. and 7 luxurious penthouses sprawling across 8500 sq. ft., this project redefines privacy and individuality. The property offers stunning views of lush 18-acre courtyard territory and urban downtown landscapes.

## Standout Features
- Boutique residential project with limited units for exclusivity.
- Spacious apartments with wrap-around balcony decks and planted parapets creating 'gardens-in-the-sky'.
- Glass window edifices ensuring ample natural sunlight in living spaces.
- Overlooks 18 acres of lush courtyard territory and urban downtown views.
- Designed to prioritize privacy with only 1 or 2 apartments per floor.
