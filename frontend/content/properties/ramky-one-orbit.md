# Ramky One Orbit

## Overview
Ramky One Orbit is a premium residential project located in Nallagandla, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.25 acres, this gated community offers a perfect blend of peaceful living and vibrant activities. With 518 luxury units available in 2 & 3 BHK configurations, Ramky One Orbit is designed to cater to the lifestyle aspirations of modern families.

## Standout Features
- Located in the thriving area of Nallagandla, Hyderabad, close to Gachibowli.
- Comprises 4 towers with a mix of G+14 and G+11 floors.
- Offers 2 & 3 BHK luxury apartments with sizes ranging from 1180 to 1855 sq. ft.
- Proximity to IT hubs, reputed schools, hospitals, and shopping centers.
- Thoughtfully designed master plan with landscaped gardens and open spaces.

## Location Highlights
- Located just 30 minutes from ORR Phase I Nanakramguda Junction.
- Walking distance to Vijetha Supermarket and Rythu Bazaar.
