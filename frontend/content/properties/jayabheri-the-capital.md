# Jayabheri The Capital

## Overview
Jayabheri The Capital is a premium residential project located near Amravati, the new capital of Andhra Pradesh. Spread across 7 acres, this luxury gated community offers a blend of modern architecture, lifestyle amenities, and a strategic location close to Benz Circle. Designed to provide a high standard of living, the project features spacious apartments, landscaped gardens, and a vibrant community atmosphere.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Located near Amravati, the emerging capital of Andhra Pradesh, and just a 10-minute drive from Benz Circle.
- **Luxury Living**: A gated community with stately apartments and world-class amenities.
- **Expansive Layout**: Spread over 7 acres with 5 residential blocks, each featuring 15 floors.
- **Ready to Occupy**: Move-in ready homes for immediate possession.
- **RERA Approved**: Registered under RERA with registration number P07110030011.
