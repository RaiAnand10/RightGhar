# My Home Apas

![My Home Apas Banner](https://www.myhomeconstructions.com/my-home-apas/assets-avali/my-home-apas-mobile.webp)

## Overview

My Home Apas is a luxurious residential project located in the serene neighborhood of Kokapet, Hyderabad. Overlooking the tranquil Kokapet Lake and Osman Sagar, this project offers a harmonious blend of sustainable living and modern comforts. With spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments, My Home Apas redefines urban living with its thoughtful design and world-class amenities.

## Standout Features

- **Prime Location**: Nestled in Kokapet, with excellent connectivity to Gachibowli, Wipro Junction, and the Outer Ring Road (ORR).
- **Luxury Living**: Spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments ranging from 2765 to 3860 Sq. Ft.
- **Expansive Project Area**: Spread across 13.52 acres with 81.6% open space.
- **Sky-High Towers**: 6 towers with 44 floors each, offering breathtaking views.
- **Massive Clubhouse**: A 72,000 SFT clubhouse with modern facilities for leisure and recreation.
- **Sustainability**: Designed to promote a healthy and eco-friendly lifestyle.
