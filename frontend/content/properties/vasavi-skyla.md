# Vasavi Skyla

## Overview
Discover a luxurious lifestyle at Vasavi Skyla, a premium gated community located in Kondapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 6.23 acres, this project offers thoughtfully designed 3, 4 & 5 BHK apartments and exclusive sky villas. With modern amenities, expansive open spaces, and a 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse, Vasavi Skyla redefines urban living.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Kondapur, Hyderabad, with excellent connectivity to metro stations, hospitals, schools, and IT hubs.
- Spacious 3, 4 & 5 BHK apartments and sky villas with panoramic city views.
- 75% open space ensuring a serene and green environment.
- RERA-approved project with possession scheduled for December 2026.
- World-class clubhouse spanning 50,000 sq. ft. for recreation and leisure.
