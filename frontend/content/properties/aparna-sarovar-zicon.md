# Aparna Sarovar Zicon

## Overview
Aparna Sarovar Zicon is a premium gated community located in Nallagandla, Hyderabad. Spanning 25.5 acres, this project offers modern 2, 3, and 4 BHK apartments designed for luxury and comfort. With 14 towers and 3024 units, it combines spacious living with seamless connectivity to key areas in Hyderabad.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Nallagandla, close to Gachibowli and HITEC City.
- **Luxury Clubhouse**: 82,313 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with 30+ premium amenities.
- **Open Spaces**: 80% open space for greenery, sunlight, and fresh air.
- **Connectivity**: Quick access to ORR, MMTS, and major IT hubs like TCS, Wipro, and Polaris.
- **Scenic Views**: Overlooks Nallagandla Lake and the green HCU campus.
- **Nearby Schools**: Close to CHIREC, DPS, Oakridge, and other prestigious schools.
