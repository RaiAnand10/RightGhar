# Vertex Viraat

## Overview
Vertex Viraat is a premium gated community located in Miyapur, Hyderabad, designed to redefine urban living with its thoughtfully crafted 2 & 3 BHK Xtra Edge Homes. Spread across 8.7 acres, this project offers a blend of modern conveniences and a vibrant community atmosphere, making it an ideal choice for families and investors alike.

## Standout Features
- Located near National Highway 65 for seamless connectivity.
- Easy access to metro stations for convenient transportation.
- Just 30 minutes' drive to major IT hubs.
- Premium gated community offering enhanced security and lifestyle.
- Close proximity to top hospitals and shopping malls.
- Phase 1 completed with 3 towers of 30 floors; Phase 2 construction progressing rapidly.
