# Hallmark Sunnyside

## Overview
Hallmark Sunnyside is a premium residential project located in the serene locality of Manchirevula, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.7 acres, this thoughtfully designed community offers 2 and 3 BHK luxury apartments with modern amenities and lush green surroundings. With a focus on healthy living and community bonding, Sunnyside is the perfect destination for families seeking a harmonious lifestyle.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Manchirevula, close to Gandipet and Outer Ring Road.
- Spacious 2 and 3 BHK apartments designed for luxury and comfort.
- G+9 floors across 5 blocks, ensuring privacy and community living.
- 22,000 Sq. Ft. world-class clubhouse with state-of-the-art facilities.
- Proximity to IT hubs like HITEC City and Gachibowli, ensuring excellent connectivity.
