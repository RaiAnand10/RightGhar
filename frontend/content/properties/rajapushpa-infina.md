# Rajapushpa Infina

## Overview
Rajapushpa Infina is a luxury residential project located in the serene locality of Manchirevula, Hyderabad. Spread across 12.96 acres, this gated community offers meticulously designed 3 & 4 BHK apartments with world-class amenities and breathtaking views. With its strategic location near the Financial District, it promises a lifestyle of convenience and sophistication.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Manchirevula, near the Financial District, Gachibowli, and Hitech City.
- High-rise towers with 55 floors offering panoramic views of the ORR and Chilkur Reserve Forest.
- Spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments ranging from 3080 to 5725 Sq. Ft.
- 69,000 Sq. Ft. state-of-the-art clubhouse with luxurious amenities.
- Stilt-level amenities spanning 80,000 Sq. Ft. for recreation and leisure.
- Proximity to top schools, hospitals, and IT hubs.
