# Jayabheri The Nirvana

## Overview
Jayabheri The Nirvana is a premium residential project located in the heart of Gachibowli, Hyderabad. Offering ready-to-move-in 3 BHK apartments, this project combines luxury, environmental sustainability, and modern living. With a sprawling 7-acre gated layout, 79% open space, and a 49,000 sq. ft. clubhouse, it is designed to provide a harmonious and comfortable lifestyle.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Gachibowli, Hyderabad, near Financial District and IT hubs.
- 7-acre gated community with 79% open space.
- 693 high-end 3 BHK apartments with sizes ranging from 1885 to 2225 sq. ft.
- Five towers with 29 floors each, featuring entrance lobbies and centralized gas supply.
- Lavish 49,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art amenities.
- Ready-to-move-in homes with OC received and no GST applicable.
- Prices starting from ₹2.45 crore onwards.
