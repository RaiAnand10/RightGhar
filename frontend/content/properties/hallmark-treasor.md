# Hallmark Treasor

## Overview
Hallmark Treasor is a luxurious high-rise residential project located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spanning 4.62 acres, this development offers 3 & 4 BHK apartments and duplex flats, designed with cutting-edge MIVAN construction technology for precision and durability. With G+29 floors and a world-class clubhouse, Hallmark Treasor sets a new benchmark for upscale living.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Kokapet, Hyderabad, with excellent connectivity to IT hubs and major landmarks.
- G+29 high-rise structure with wide-open balconies offering stunning views of greenery and water bodies.
- Advanced MIVAN engineering technology ensuring accuracy and speedy construction.
- 35,000 Sq. Ft. world-class clubhouse with premium facilities.
- Strategically located near Outer Ring Road (ORR) for seamless access to HITEC City, Gachibowli, and the airport.
