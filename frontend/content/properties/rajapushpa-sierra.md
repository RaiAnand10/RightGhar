# Rajapushpa Sierra

## Overview
Rajapushpa Sierra is a premium residential project located in Tellapur, Hyderabad, within the expansive Rajapushpa Lifestyle City. Spread across 21.1 acres with 76% open spaces, it offers meticulously designed 2 & 3 BHK residences that blend contemporary sophistication, comfort, and style. With 9 iconic towers and 3,537 apartments, Sierra redefines luxury living in one of Hyderabad's most sought-after neighborhoods.
