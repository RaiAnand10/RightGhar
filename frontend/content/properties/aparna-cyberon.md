# Aparna CyberOn

## Overview
Aparna CyberOn is a premium residential project located in Osman Nagar, Hyderabad. Spread across 2.8 acres, this thoughtfully designed community offers spacious 2.5 and 3 BHK apartments with modern amenities and scenic views. Positioned near the IT corridor, it combines urban accessibility with serene living.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located near West Hyderabad's IT corridor.
- 2 towers with 10 floors, offering a total of 160 units.
- Clubhouse spanning 14,718 Sq. Ft. with 20+ lifestyle amenities.
- Surrounded by greenery and offers views of Osman Nagar Lake.
- Minutes from ORR Exit 2, Lingampally Railway Station, and Financial District.
- Located opposite a proposed 100 Ft. HMDA-approved road and future IT development land.
