# Lansum Elena Residences

## Overview
Lansum Elena Residences is a thoughtfully designed residential project located in the serene Kokapet area of Hyderabad. This project emphasizes a balanced lifestyle with a focus on relaxation, wellness, and community living. With its modern amenities and strategic location, it offers the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Kokapet, Hyderabad, near the Outer Ring Road (ORR) Exit 1.
- A fully-equipped 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art facilities.
- Emphasis on slow living with spacious layouts, natural light, and seamless connection to nature.
- Proximity to top schools, hospitals, and the Financial District.
- Designed to nurture mind, body, and soul with wellness-focused amenities.

## Amenities
### Clubhouse
- State-of-the-art gym with cardio and strength training equipment.
- Group workout studio for Zumba, yoga, and Pilates.
- Indoor temperature-controlled pool and terrace pool.
- Banquet halls and youth party hall for celebrations.
- Guest rooms for visiting family and friends.
- Media creation studio, music room, and recording studio.
- Library and co-working space for inspiration and focus.

### Outdoor
- Lush tropical groves, sensory gardens, and walking trails.
- Open-air amphitheatre and picnic spots for community gatherings.
- Vibrant playgrounds and multi-purpose courts for recreation.
- Stargazing decks and green meditative spaces for tranquility.

### Wellness & Recreation
- Open-air yoga decks and meditation zones.
- Energizing fitness areas and invigorating swimming pools.
- Lotus ponds and seating decks overlooking calming waterscapes.

### Sports & Games
- Indoor squash and badminton courts.
- Table tennis, pool, and air hockey facilities.
- Retro arcade room and karaoke for entertainment.

## Location Highlights
- Located in Kokapet, Hyderabad, away from the city's hustle yet close to modern conveniences.
- **2 minutes** from ORR Exit 1, ensuring seamless connectivity.
- **25 minutes** to Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.
- **5 minutes** to the Financial District.
- **10 minutes** to top international schools like Oakridge International and The Shri Ram Universal.
- Proximity to renowned hospitals such as Continental and AIG.
- Nearby luxury dining options like Zega at The Sheraton and Mrs. Mage at The Hyatt.
- Easy access to Kokapet Lake and other recreational spots.

Lansum Elena Residences offers a unique opportunity to embrace a tranquil yet connected lifestyle, making it an ideal choice for families and professionals alike.
