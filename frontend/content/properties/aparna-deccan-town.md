# Aparna Deccan Town

## Overview
Aparna Deccan Town is a landmark township located in the thriving Gopanpally-Gachibowli area of Hyderabad. Spanning 24.9 acres, this expansive project features four high-rise luxury gated apartment communities offering 3 and 4 BHK configurations, alongside an exclusive enclave of 5 BHK uber-luxury bungalows. Designed to provide a harmonious blend of nature and urban connectivity, Aparna Deccan Town is the township of tomorrow.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Gopanpally-Gachibowli, Hyderabad, near major IT hubs and commercial centers.
- Four luxury gated apartment communities and one enclave of premium bungalows.
- High-street retail and multiplex for entertainment and shopping.
- Spacious unit sizes ranging from 1478 to 10156 Sq. Ft.
- Towering structures with G+44 floors across 9 towers.
