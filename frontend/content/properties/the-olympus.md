# The Olympus

## Overview
The Olympus by Sumadhura Realty is an ultra-luxury residential project located in the Financial District, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.06 acres, it features two iconic 44-story towers offering 3 & 4 BHK apartments starting from ₹2.26 Cr onwards. Designed to redefine luxury living, this project combines architectural brilliance with world-class amenities.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in the Financial District, Hyderabad, close to Fortune 500 companies and major schools.
- **Iconic Design**: Twin towers soaring 44 stories into the skyline, offering panoramic city views.
- **Exclusive Skyvillas**: Limited-edition penthouses with double-height living spaces and private terraces.
- **Thoughtful Interiors**: Floor-to-ceiling windows, premium finishes, and maximized natural light.
- **World-Class Amenities**: Resort-style luxuries including an infinity pool, fitness center, and meditation gardens.
