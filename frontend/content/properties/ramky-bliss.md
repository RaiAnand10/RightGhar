# Ramky Bliss

## Overview
Ramky Bliss is a premium villa plotting project by Ramky Estates, spread across 12.36 acres in Rajapur, Hyderabad. This MUDA-approved development offers 130 villa plots ranging from 180 to 802 square yards, designed with essential infrastructure like underground utilities, stormwater drainage, and electricity connections, making it an ideal choice for building your dream home.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located in Rajapur, abutting the 6-lane Hyderabad to Bengaluru National Highway 44.
- Just a 45-minute drive from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.
- Proximity to key hubs like Pollepally SEZ and TSIIC – Pharma SEZ.
- MUDA-approved villa plots with ready-to-build infrastructure.
- Sizes ranging from 180 to 802 square yards, catering to diverse needs.

## Amenities
