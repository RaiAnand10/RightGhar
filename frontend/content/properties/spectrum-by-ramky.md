# Spectrum by Ramky

## Overview
Spectrum by Ramky is a premium gated community located in Korremula, East Hyderabad. Spread across 13.38 acres, this luxurious development comprises 151 meticulously designed 4 BHK triplex villas, offering a perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and sustainability. With villa plots ranging from 181 to 467 sq. yds. and super built-up areas between 2,472 and 2,913 sq.ft., Spectrum provides a sophisticated lifestyle for discerning homeowners.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located just 5 minutes from the ORR, near Infosys SEZ and Raheja Mindspace.
- Exclusive 4 BHK triplex villas with 10-ft ceiling heights, dedicated car parking, EV charging points, and solar power provisions.
- Smart living features including 1 kW power backup for each villa.
- A sprawling 16,830 sq.ft. clubhouse with world-class recreational facilities.
- 14 themed parks, mini golf, yoga lawns, party lawns, pet parks, and children’s play zones.
