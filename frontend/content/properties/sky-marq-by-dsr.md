# Sky Marq by DSR

## Overview
Sky Marq by DSR is a luxurious residential project located in the heart of Hyderabad. Spread across 7.11 acres, this architectural marvel features 664 premium apartments with expansive unit sizes ranging from 3999 to 5454 sq. ft. Designed to redefine urban living, Sky Marq offers unparalleled views, exquisite interiors, and world-class amenities.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located on the Hyderabad Growth Corridor, ensuring seamless connectivity.
- One of the tallest residential towers in Hyderabad with 45 levels.
- Premium apartments with spacious layouts and designer interiors.
- Easy access to the IT hub and Rajiv Gandhi International Airport.
- RERA-approved project with registration number P02400005887.
