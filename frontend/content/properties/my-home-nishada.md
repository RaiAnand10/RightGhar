# My Home Nishada

## Overview
My Home Nishada is a resort-themed residential project located in Kokapet, Hyderabad. Spanning 16.68 acres, this luxurious development features 8 sky-high towers with 1398 ultra-spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments. Designed to offer an elevated lifestyle, the project boasts world-class amenities, abundant greenery, and seamless connectivity to major business hubs.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Kokapet, with easy access to Hitech City, Financial District, and Outer Ring Road.
- **Luxurious Living**: 3 & 4 BHK apartments ranging from 3450 to 4617 sq. ft.

- **Exclusive Clubhouse**: 1 Lakh Sq. Ft. clubhouse with premium amenities.
