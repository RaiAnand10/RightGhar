# Candeur Crescent

## Overview
Candeur Crescent is a premium gated community located in Serilingampally, Hyderabad. Spread across 5.5 acres, this luxurious residential project features 5 towers with 50 floors each, offering spacious 2.5 and 3 BHK apartments. Designed for modern living, the project combines functionality, style, and comfort, making it an ideal choice for families and professionals alike.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Serilingampally, close to IT hubs like Gachibowli and Hitec City.
- **Architectural Excellence**: Inspired by the natural arc of a crescent, ensuring harmony and sophistication.
- **High-Rise Living**: 50 floors offering panoramic city views.
- **Spacious Layouts**: Apartments ranging from 1333 to 2656 Sq. Ft.
- **Exclusive Clubhouse**: A massive 62,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with world-class amenities.
- **Open Spaces**: 80% corner apartments for privacy, natural light, and ventilation.
- **Possession Timeline**: Ready by December 2025.
