# Hallmark Skyrena

## Overview
Hallmark Skyrena is a premium residential project located in the serene and well-connected locality of Narsingi, Hyderabad. Spread across 6.5 acres with 70% open space, the project offers spacious 2 & 3 BHK homes designed for modern living. With a world-class 37,000 sq. ft. clubhouse and the first-ever Skywalk in Hyderabad, Skyrena redefines urban living by combining city convenience with natural tranquility.

## Standout Features
- Located in Narsingi, a prime area with excellent connectivity to IT hubs like Gachibowli and HITEC City.
- First-ever Skywalk in Hyderabad, offering a unique lifestyle experience 147 feet above the ground.
- 70% open space for a serene and nature-inspired living environment.
- World-class 37,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art amenities.
- Proximity to key landmarks such as Kokapet, Financial District, and ORR.
