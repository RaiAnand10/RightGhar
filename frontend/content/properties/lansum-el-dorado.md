# Lansum El-Dorado

## Overview
Lansum El-Dorado is a premium residential project located in Narsingi, Hyderabad, just minutes away from the Financial District. Spread across 4 acres, the project features two 40-floor towers offering 872 luxurious 3 BHK apartments. With unit sizes ranging from 1860 to 2180 Sq. Ft. and prices starting at ₹2.1 Cr, El-Dorado redefines opulent living with state-of-the-art amenities and a prime location.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Narsingi, just 14 minutes from the Financial District, close to IT/ITES hubs like Gachibowli and HITECH City.
- **Architectural Excellence**: Two 40-floor towers with biophilic design, blending luxury and nature.
- **Exclusive Clubhouse**: A 50,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with 6 awe-inspiring levels of amenities.
- **Sky-Deck & Skywalk**: Stunning 30th-floor sky deck and all-weather elevated skywalk offering panoramic city views.
- **Spacious Apartments**: Premium 3 BHK units ranging from 1860 to 2180 Sq. Ft.
