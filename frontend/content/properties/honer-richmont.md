# Honer Richmont

## Overview
Honer Richmont is a luxurious residential community featuring 142 designer boutique mansions located at Green Hills Road, near HITEC City, Hyderabad. Spanning 28.4 acres, this premium project offers 5 BHK villas designed to elevate your lifestyle with world-class amenities, lush green surroundings, and seamless connectivity.

## Standout Features
- Prime location near HITEC City, Hyderabad, ensuring excellent connectivity.
- Exclusive 5 BHK boutique mansions with spacious layouts ranging from 6787 to 10187 Sq. Ft.
- Expansive 28.4-acre development with 2.5 acres of central park.
- 50,000 Sq. Ft. luxe clubhouse with premium amenities.
- Iconic architectural design featuring double-height drawing rooms and private elevators.
