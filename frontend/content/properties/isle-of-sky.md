# Isle of Sky

## Overview
Isle of Sky by Myscape redefines luxury living with its exclusive offering of just 28 forested apartments, each occupying an entire floor in a single tower. This project combines privacy, greenery, and breathtaking views, creating a unique villa-in-the-sky experience.

## Standout Features
- **Exclusivity**: Only 28 apartments, one per floor, ensuring privacy and exclusivity.
- **Green Living**: Vertical garden with 250 trees, including some reaching up to 12 feet.
- **Panoramic Views**: Stunning 360-degree views from all sides of the apartment.
- **Lifestyle Amenities**: Grand lobby, infinity pool, and rooftop gym for a luxurious lifestyle.
- **Spacious Design**: Each apartment features 4 expansive bedrooms.
