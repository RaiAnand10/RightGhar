# Honer Vivantis

## Overview
Honer Vivantis is a premium residential project located in the serene locality of Gopanpally, Gachibowli. Spread across 11 acres, this thoughtfully designed gated community offers 2 & 3 BHK apartments with unit sizes ranging from 1290 to 1725 sq. ft. Completed in 2019, Honer Vivantis combines luxury, comfort, and sustainability, making it an ideal choice for modern living.

## Standout Features
- Prime location in Gopanpally, Gachibowli, offering excellent connectivity.
- Spread across 11 acres with 76% open spaces for a tranquil living experience.
- 10 towers with 12 floors each, housing a total of 760 units.
- Spacious 2 & 3 BHK apartments with premium finishes and modern layouts.
- A perfect blend of natural serenity and urban convenience.
