# Som Boulevard

## Overview
Som Boulevard is a premium villa project located in Mokila, Hyderabad, spread across an expansive 40 acres of serene greenery. Offering 270 aesthetically designed 4 BHK villas, this project is a perfect blend of modern architecture and nature, providing a luxurious and tranquil living experience. With innovative construction and thoughtful design, Som Boulevard ensures a lifestyle of comfort and elegance.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Mokila, just off the Outer Ring Road, ensuring excellent connectivity to key areas of Hyderabad.
- **Spacious Villas**: 4 BHK villas with sizes ranging from 300 to 475 Sq. Yds., featuring modern layouts and luxurious finishes.
- **Exclusive Clubhouse**: A sprawling 48,000 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art facilities.
- **Nature-Focused Living**: Landscaped deck areas, water features, and ample green spaces for a serene environment.
- **Innovative Design**: Ground + 2 floors with wooden flooring in the master bedroom and high ceiling heights (Ground: 11’6”, First & Second: 11’).
