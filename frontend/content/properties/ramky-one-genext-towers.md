# Ramky One Genext Towers

## Overview
Ramky One Genext Towers is a thoughtfully planned residential project located in Uppal, Hyderabad. Designed to cater to the needs of multiple generations, this gated community offers a serene environment, fostering relationships and creating lasting memories. With modern amenities and a vibrant atmosphere, it promises a lifestyle of comfort and pride.

## Standout Features
- Located in Uppal, the thriving hub of East Hyderabad.
- Excellent connectivity with proximity to NGRI Metro Station, Secunderabad Railway Station, and Hyderabad International Airport.
- Spread across 5.7 acres with 4 towers and 596 luxury units.
- Offers 2 & 3 BHK apartments with spacious layouts ranging from 1310 to 1860 Sq. Ft.
- Designed for multi-generational living with a focus on community and modern living.
