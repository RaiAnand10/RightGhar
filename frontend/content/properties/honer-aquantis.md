# Honer Aquantis

## Overview
Honer Aquantis is a premium IGBC Pre-Certified residential project located in the serene locality of Gopanpally, Gachibowli, Hyderabad. Spread across 10.5 acres with 81% open space, this gated community offers ready-to-move 2 & 3 BHK apartments, along with exclusive 4 BHK duplexes. With 6 towers, 30 floors, and a 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse, Honer Aquantis is designed to provide a luxurious and eco-friendly lifestyle.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Gopanpally, near the IT Hub and Financial District of Gachibowli.
- **Spacious Living**: Offers 2 & 3 BHK apartments and limited edition 4 BHK duplexes.
- **Expansive Area**: Spanning 10.5 acres with 81% open spaces, ensuring a serene and green environment.
- **Modern Infrastructure**: 6 towers with G+30 floors, providing stunning views and ample natural light.
- **Eco-Friendly Design**: IGBC Pre-Certified Green Project with sustainable features.
- **State-of-the-Art Clubhouse**: A massive 50,000 sq. ft. clubhouse with premium amenities.
- **EV Charging Stations**: Supporting sustainable living with electric vehicle charging facilities.
- **Proximity to IT Hub**: Just a 10-minute drive to the Financial District.
