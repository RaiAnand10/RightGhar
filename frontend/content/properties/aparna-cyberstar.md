# Aparna CyberStar

## Overview
Aparna CyberStar is a premium gated community located in Osman Nagar, Hyderabad. Spread across 2.37 acres, this project offers 272 thoughtfully designed apartments in configurations of 2, 2.5, and 3 BHK. With its strategic location near the IT corridor and excellent connectivity, Aparna CyberStar is poised to deliver a modern lifestyle with serene surroundings.

## Standout Features
- Strategically located in Osman Nagar, Hyderabad, offering excellent connectivity to Kollur, Gachibowli, HITEC City, and the Financial District.
- Direct access to the Outer Ring Road (ORR) and Gopanpally Junction.
- Opposite a proposed 100 Ft. HMDA-approved road.
- Part of the Aparna Cyber4 Hub, a premium urban township.
- Close proximity to top schools, hospitals, and essential services.
- 16,835 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with 20+ premium amenities.
- TSIIC land across the development earmarked for IT developments.
