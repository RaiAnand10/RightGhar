# Rajapushpa Provincia

## Overview
Rajapushpa Provincia is a premium high-rise residential project located in Narsingi, Hyderabad. Spread across 23.75 acres, it offers 2 & 3 BHK lifestyle apartments with world-class amenities, abundant open spaces, and a vibrant community. Designed to redefine luxury living, this project ensures a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and connectivity.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Narsingi, with excellent connectivity to IT hubs, schools, and hospitals.
- **Spacious Living**: Unit sizes ranging from 1370 to 2660 Sq. Ft.
- **Modern Architecture**: 11 towers with G+39 floors.
- **Extensive Clubhouses**: Two clubhouses spanning 1.5 lakh sq. ft. with premium facilities.
- **Eco-Friendly Features**: Electric car charging provision for every apartment.
- **Abundant Open Spaces**: Lush green landscapes and healing gardens.

## Location Highlights
- **Connectivity**: Easy access to Outer Ring Road (ORR), ensuring seamless travel to IT hubs like Gachibowli and Financial District.
- **Nearby Landmarks**: Close proximity to reputed schools, hospitals, and shopping malls.
- **Transportation**: Well-connected by road with public transport options available.
- **Surroundings**: Located amidst serene greenery and water bodies, offering a tranquil living environment.
