# Aparna Sarovar Towers

## Overview
Aparna Sarovar Towers is a premium high-rise residential project located in Nallagandla, Hyderabad. Featuring the tallest towers in the area, this project offers luxurious 3 and 4 BHK apartments with breathtaking views of the lush HCU campus and beyond. Spread across 5.25 acres, the project is designed to provide a perfect blend of modern living and natural surroundings.

## Standout Features
- **Strategic Location**: Situated on Nallagandla High Street with excellent connectivity to Gachibowli, Financial District, and HITEC City.
- **Tallest Towers in Nallagandla**: Offering panoramic views of the cityscape and the HCU campus.
- **Proximity to Conveniences**: Located near reputed schools, hospitals, supermarkets, and Aparna NEO Mall.
- **Exclusive Design**: Apartments with an extra maid room and three car parks per unit.
- **Premium Amenities**: A grand 52,932 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with over 40 premium amenities.
- **Sustainable Living**: A community designed to keep you close to nature.
