# GHR Titania

## Overview
GHR Titania by GHR Infra is a premium residential project located in Kondapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 4.7 acres, this gated community offers a blend of smart living and sustainable lifestyle with luxurious 2, 2.5, and 3 BHK apartments. Designed with a focus on sustainability and technology, GHR Titania redefines modern living.

## Standout Features
- Located in Kondapur, a prime residential area in Hyderabad.
- IGBC Pre-Certified Gold Rated project for sustainability.
- Spacious apartments with sizes ranging from 1270 to 2509 Sq. Ft.
- 23,700 Sq. Ft. clubhouse with premium amenities.
- Smart home features like voice control, remote access, and automation.
- Rooftop solar power and energy-efficient installations.
- EV charging stations and water-efficient fixtures.
