# Aparna Newlands

## Overview
Aparna Newlands is a premium gated community project located in Tellapur, Hyderabad. Spread across 14.7 acres, this luxurious development offers 2.5 and 3 BHK apartments designed for a modern lifestyle. With 8 high-rise towers, 42 floors, and 1976 units, Aparna Newlands combines comfort, convenience, and connectivity to create an urban oasis.

## Standout Features
- **Prime Location**: Situated in Tellapur, Hyderabad, with excellent connectivity to IT hubs, schools, and hospitals.
- **Spacious Clubhouse**: A 72,865 sq. ft. clubhouse with state-of-the-art amenities.
- **Zigzag Layout**: Enhanced ventilation and natural light for every unit.
- **Sustainability Features**: Dedicated EV charging points and ample green spaces.
- **Seamless Connectivity**: 100 ft. road to Osman Nagar/ORR Service Road and 60 ft. road to Gopanpally & Financial District.
- **Ample Parking**: Three cellars dedicated to car parking.
