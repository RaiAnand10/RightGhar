# CINQ by Raghava

## Overview
CINQ by Raghava is a luxurious residential project located in the heart of Hyderabad’s Financial District. With five majestic towers rising 61 floors high, this project is a perfect blend of modern architecture and serene landscapes. Designed to create a harmonious living experience, CINQ offers spacious 4 BHK apartments with maid rooms, ensuring privacy and comfort for its residents.

## Standout Features
- Located in the prestigious Financial District of Hyderabad, offering seamless connectivity to key areas.
- Five elegantly designed towers with 61 floors each, providing breathtaking views of the city.
- Spacious 4 BHK apartments with a generous size of 3600 sq. ft. per unit.
- Built on a sprawling 7.19-acre property, offering a perfect balance of nature and modern living.
- Proximity to business hubs, international schools, healthcare facilities, and entertainment zones.
